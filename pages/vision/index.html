The Bitcoin blockchain from space. No internet required.

The NetixSol Satellite network broadcasts the Bitcoin blockchain around the world 24/7 for free, protecting against network interruptions and providing areas without reliable internet connections with the opportunity to use Bitcoin.

Contact us for more details

Why Blockchain?

Internet Not Required
NetixSol Satellite broadcasts the Bitcoin blockchain to the entire planet via satellite, reducing Bitcoins dependency on internet access. Everyone in the world now has an opportunity to use Bitcoin.
Massive Cost Savings
By eliminating cost barriers, people can now receive blocks at no cost, which allows more people to utilize Bitcoin and participate in the Bitcoin network.
Network Stability
NetixSol Satellite provides an alternative method for receiving the Bitcoin blockchain that is not affected by connection failure. This protects users against network interruptions and prevents any full node from becoming isolated or partitioned.
